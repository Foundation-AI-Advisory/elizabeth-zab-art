About the show

One of Northeast Ohio’s long-running art events.

Boston Mills Artfest brings together artists from across the country for two weekends of fine art, live music, food, craft beer, wine, and a garden space — all in a relaxed outdoor setting near Cuyahoga Valley National Park. Each weekend has its own group of artists, so the show is a little different every time you go.

Liz is showing the first weekend, June 27–28. The second weekend, July 4–5, features a separate lineup of artists.

Why come in person

Poured paint is different in person.

Liz’s work is built from movement, color, and layered decisions that are hard to read on a screen. Up close, you get a truer sense of the depth, finish, scale, and surface of each piece.
